An undergraduate degree is the starting point to your future. Consider it the academic foundation. It’s typically the first degree you can work on at the university level.

Your undergraduate degree is about exploration and discovery. Expand your thinking with a variety of courses, both through your program and through electives. No matter what program you choose, from Music to Science, you’ll work on critical skills for the future like critical thinking, communication and citizenship.

The Choice Is Yours

It’s all about finding something you’re passionate about. Truth is, you might not know right now. That’s okay. The common first year at Western lets you explore.

Not sure where to start? Explore the undergraduate faculties and programs at Western. Titles with a * mean they are second-entry programs. This means you have to complete an undergraduate degree before starting them.
